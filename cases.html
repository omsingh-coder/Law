Landmark Constitutional Cases

1. Kesavananda Bharati v. State of Kerala

Case that introduced the Basic Structure Doctrine in Indian Constitution.

2. Maneka Gandhi v. Union of India

Expanded the interpretation of Article 21: Life and Personal Liberty.

3. Golak Nath v. State of Punjab

Held that Parliament cannot amend Fundamental Rights.

4. Minerva Mills v. Union of India

Reaffirmed balance between Fundamental Rights and DPSPs.

5. Vishaka v. State of Rajasthan

Laid down guidelines against sexual harassment at workplace.

6. M.C. Mehta v. Union of India

Landmark environmental law case on pollution control.

7. Indira Sawhney v. Union of India

Upheld 27% reservation for OBCs and defined creamy layer.

8. Shah Bano Case

Debated maintenance rights for Muslim women post-divorce.

9. Naz Foundation v. NCT Delhi

Challenged constitutionality of Section 377 of IPC.

10. Navtej Singh Johar v. Union of India

Decriminalized homosexuality in India.
